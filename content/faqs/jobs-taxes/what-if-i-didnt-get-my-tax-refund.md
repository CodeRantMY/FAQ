If you haven't received a tax refund, follow the steps below:

* First, go to this link: https://mytax.hasil.gov.my/.
* Perform self-identification and log in.
* Check your inbox to see if you have any attachments.
* If your issue persists, you can engage in a live chat with the LHDN (Inland Revenue Board of Malaysia).
* If your passport number is different from the one registered with LHDN, you can chat with LHDN to update your new passport number.
* The passport number provided in your EA-2021 will be followed by LHDN for tax refund purposes. Your bank account should also have the same passport number.
* If you have any other questions, I'm here to assist you.
