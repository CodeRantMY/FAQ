1. Release letter from hospital
2. JPN form provided by hospital, properly filled up
3. Passport \+ visa (original \+ copies)
4. Dr. appointment card of the mother (original \+ copy)
5. Marriage cert CTC/original

*Note \- If you want binte/bin for baby name, go directly to Putrajaya JPN. Other JPNs will reject.*
Once you get the Birth Certificate from JPN, Copy it 3 and get them attested from the
same JPN. You also need to get Birth Certificate attested from Foreign Ministry of Malaysia, Putrajaya, RM20 for each copy.
