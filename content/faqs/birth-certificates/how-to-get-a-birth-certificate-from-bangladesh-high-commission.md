For the babies born in Malaysia, you can’t generate the birth certificate from Bangladesh anymore\!

You need to do it via the Bangladesh Embassy in KL. But the process is not started yet. Maybe need 1/2 more weeks. And they’re not familiar with the application process. So, if you want to do that \- prepare yourself for at least 4 hours to complete the process (if you want to get it in a day).

Docs needed:
1\. Marriage certificate
2\. Father’s and Mother’s passport
3\. Father’s and Mother’s Birth Certificate, both have to be online.
4\. Father’s and Mother’s visa copy
5\. Filled the form: [https://bdris.gov.bd/br/application](https://bdris.gov.bd/br/application) try to print it, if can’t get the application ID screenshot and print it.

6\. JPN birth cert of the baby attested from Foreign Ministry of Malaysia, Putrajaya
7\. 5rm in yellow payslip
8\. You can submit the application from 10am to 1pm and collect it from 4pm to 5pm on the same day.
