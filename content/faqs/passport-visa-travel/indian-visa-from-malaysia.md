Allow a timeframe of 5-6 days for the process.
Visit the link provided (https://indianvisaonline.gov.in/visa/Registration) for more information.
Note that it usually takes 5/6 working days.
Submit the request in advance.
When visiting the visa center to submit the documents online, submit the request on that day.

Although not mandatory, consider getting a printed bank statement for the past 6 months.
This may be useful for your application.
