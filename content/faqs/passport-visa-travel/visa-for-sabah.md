* Visit the Sabah government's official website or use the following URL: https://phednp.wixsite.com/website.
* On the website, look for a button or link that says "Special Approval Form." Click on it.
* You will be redirected to a Google Form. Access the form using the following link: [https://tinyurl.com/borangfeb](https://tinyurl.com/borangfeb)
* Fill out the Special Approval Form with all the required details. Make sure to provide accurate information.
* Once you have completed the form, gather the necessary documents for submission. These include:
  * Employment letter on office letterhead
  * Copy of your passport
  * Copy of your employment pass (EP)
  * Copy of your dependent pass (DP)
  * Copy of your Kuala Lumpur (KL) home address
  * Copy of your marriage certificate
  * Confirmed flight tickets (including return tickets, which are mandatory)
  * Screenshots of your MySJ profile as proof of vaccination
* Compose an email to MohdRafi.Jubahar@sabah.gov.my. Attach all the required documents to the email. In the email, provide a clear and concise explanation of your purpose for visiting Sabah and the dates of your intended entry and exit.
* After reviewing your documents, Mohd Rafi Jubahar will reply with an official letter. The letter will include your name, passport number, date of entry into Sabah, and date of exit from Sabah.
* Once you receive the official letter, print it out and present the printed official letter at the Sabah airport as proof of your special approval.
