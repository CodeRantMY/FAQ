Monitors up to 29 inches do not require tax. However, once you reach 30 inches or above, you may have to pay tax. Some websites may provide conflicting information on this matter. Regarding whether the tax exemption applies to both TVs and monitors, it seems that the size of the screen matters more than the specific device. So, any screen below 30 inches should be exempt from tax.
